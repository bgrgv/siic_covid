E - Ventilator
This is an automatic Ambu bag pressing machine which can be used as an alternate to full-fledged ventilators to provide forced breathing to the patient who is having respiratory problems. The device operation is automated with the help of an electromechanical system. The goal is to ensure a minimum safe ventilator functionality based on clinical guidance with the lowest cost and easily manufacturable solution. The concept is to squeeze the ambu bag which is a standard medical equipment in a certain breathing pattern for the patient. This ventilator has easily customizable breathing frequency with the help of this knob from 10 beats to 30 beats per minute and breathing volume can be customized with the help of this knob from 300 ml to 450 ml. Inhale/Exhale ratio can also be customized. A buzzer is kept for intimation in case of system failures. This kill switch is used to stop the system in case of emergencies. The bag can be easily detached from the system in case system fails and operator has to manually pump the air.
By Rizel Automotive (Startup) : Currently Prototype developed, its in final stage of testing.

Kalam Protector
The Unmanned Ground Vehicle (UGV) is a robotic system that can drive and navigate itself autonomously to sterilize streets from infection.

Outdoor- This UGV can travel and disinfect up to a distance of 15km under a single charge. It has a 100 Litre storage of disinfectant liquid. It can also charge its battery and refill the disinfectant liquid automatically and repeat the designated operation round the days on its own.

Indoors- the robot can be fitter with Ultra Violette Disinfection system that this robot can navigate autonomously in the rooms and can disinfect it and return.

A solution that can work on the virus and kill them is a combination of phenol and formaldehyde in the mole ratio of 1: 1.05, through condensation polymerisation with base as catalyst. The product obtained after polymerization dissolved in water around 10% is proven to kill the virus.

Nightingale
Problem:The isolated patients in the hospitals are regularly needed to be checked and be provided timely medicines and food, take samples and disinfect. This process involves the doctors, nurses and attenders to visit them on a regular basis everyday. There is another scenario when a patient dies, the attenders, nurses and doctors are extremely skeptical even to approach the body for the further process.

Solution:The need of the hour is to aid them on their process and prevent them from coming in close contact with the isolated patients. This can be achieved by the usage of robots that does the job of delivering food and medicine and help them with their sample testing and other activities.

Applications:

1.Delivers food and medicine on automatic scheduled intervals. 2.Regularly checks the patient’s conditions visually and updates the doctors. 3.Disinfects the floor and surroundings on a scheduled interval. 4.Allow video calls that helps the doctors to communicate also pave way for the family members to still be in touch. 5.Safely take salivary samples from the patients and deliver it for testing. 6.Attend the body of the deceased patients
